IT, High Speed Application, (SSD) FAE`s role

Our customer’s ultimate success depends on our ability to solve cutting edge problems in relation to IT, High Speed Application, (SSD) industries. We value our relationships with these companies, and we depend on our Sales and Field Applications Engineering Teams to foster this ongoing partnership.
As a Field Applications Engineer, he/she will interface with our customers, and internal product development, product marketing, and sales teams.

Responsibilities

1. Engineering resource on the sales team to lead technical aspects of the sales cycle.
2. Provide technical marketing support for the development and implementation of ACP Group
3. Prepare materials and participate in product presentations.
4. Provide customer service to solve complex interconnect problems and technical inquiries.
5. Help devise strategies to differentiate our products and solutions from our competitors.
6. Collaborate with local/group/customers to meet and exceed strategic objectives (create New Business Opportunity & achieve Design Win)
7. Lead connector promotion and development (manage BU, factory), customer approval, and quality controll.
8. Collaborate with BU and factories at overseas locations to support customer needs.
