Job Summary
You will support the maintenance strategy of production facilities in Air Liquide Advanced Materials (ALAM) based in the SJ plant. You will have a strong reliability focus and develop engineering solutions to decrease reactive maintenance and increase planned preventative maintenance.


How will you contribute and Grow?
- Maintenance and improvement work in the process and utility machinery field
- Preventive maintenance & corrective maintenance schedule and plan establishment
- Implement engineering solutions.
- Conduct RCA’s and FMEA’s and support PSM
- Manage improvement projects
- Perform Life Cycle Analysis
- Implement Predictive Maintenance systems and support maintenance minimization efforts
- Review and modify plans, procedure, material usage as needed
- Develop reporting of maintenance activity, Key Performance Indicators (KPIs) and Metrics.
- Manage CAPEX & OPEX budgets
- Ensure compliance with HSES and Regulatory requirements

자격요건
